Why Us ?

Housegroom.com is not an agency and thus does not keep any maids on rolls. We are an organization providing access to all the maids available locality / speciality wise. There is no fixed amount charged by us for the maid. Customers can directly connect with the maid, conduct interview, shortlist, negotiate and hire.

Housegroom.com is primarily a location based search engine providing maids based on specialty and timing. You can save on paying any extra amount for the services which an aggregator takes apart from having a maid of your own choice. Choice of maids can be also made based on previous customers review and rating. Hence you have the complete flexibility of choosing maids of your choice.
